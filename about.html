About Harinama eva kevalam

Our mission is to bring the transcendental sound of the Holy Name to every heart across the world.

Our Story

Harinama eva kevalam was born from a simple desire – to create a digital space where anyone, anywhere in the world, can experience the peace and bliss of kirtan.

Inspired by the streets of Vrindavan, the banks of the Yamuna, and the timeless tradition of Harinam Sankirtan, we set out to build a living, breathing kirtan experience online.

Every kirtan, every mantra, every story on this platform is curated with love and devotion – to help you feel closer to Krishna, no matter where you are.

Our Values
